Biography

Charles Nii Armah Mensah Jnr born to Charles Nii Armah Mensah Snr and Elsie Evelyn Avemegah was born in Accra-Ghana on October 17,1984. He attended Seven Great Princes Academy at Dansoman, where he started to show interest in arts and acted in the then popular drama sense, By the Fireside. As a child, his father introduced him to Jamaican culture by playing Reggae records in the house and also continuously traveling with him to the UK to meet his relatives who were half Jamaicans. Shatta had to live in almost all the ghettos in Nima, Accra to survive He later moved to Jamaica to master his dancehall craft.
